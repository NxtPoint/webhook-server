How the academy model works

Coaches join free.
Players bring their own credits.

There's no per-seat academy licence to negotiate before you start. Players (or their parents) hold the subscription and the credits; coaches are invited in for free; the academy coordinates the roster.

Players pay for uploads

Each player subscribes or pays as they go. Every upload consumes their credit — never the academy's or the coach's.

Coaches are invited, free

A player invites each coach they work with. First player is free for every coach; Coach Pro unlocks unlimited linked players at $50/mo.

Running a bigger squad?

For a whole-academy rollout — onboarding help, bulk player setup, a coaching-team structure — talk to us and we'll set it up with you.

Rolling it out

From first squad to
full academy in a week.

1

Start with one squad

Pick a group — your performance players, a single age band — and have them (or their parents) create accounts. The free trial gives every player their first match analysed at no cost.

2

Players invite their coaches

From the Locker Room, each player adds the coaches who work with them. Coaches accept with one click — no card, no setup — and immediately see that player's full history.

3

Matches start flowing in

Every match the squad films — phone, club camera, tournament footage — is analysed automatically. Coaches open the portal to point-by-point breakdowns, heatmaps and trends without lifting a finger.

4

Coaches compare across the roster

Performance trending, technique scores and the AI Coach work across every linked player, so your staff can spot who's progressing, who's plateauing and what to work on next.

5

Roll it out to the whole academy

Once the first squad is humming, add the rest. Want help with bulk onboarding and a coaching-team structure across age groups? That's exactly what a quick call with us sorts out.
