What you get

Six things you didn't have
before breakfast.

Everything a touring coach's staff would prep for a pro — but for every player on your roster, ready before they're done stretching.

01

A match report from every match — even the ones you missed

Parent films on a phone. Club camera auto-uploads. Junior plays abroad. By the time you open the app, the analysis is done. Point-by-point, heatmaps, KPIs — on your phone before the player's home.

02

Trend lines that tell you what's really changing

Is their first-serve percentage actually up, or are you just remembering the good ones? Is the backhand error rate climbing since the grip change? Our performance module answers with data, not gut feel.

03

An AI coach trained on your player's data

Ask "what tactical change would give my player the biggest lift against deep returners?" and get an answer grounded in their actual patterns — not generic coaching blog content.

04

Technique analysis that catches what your eye misses

Pose estimation + kinetic chain scoring finds the technical issues before they become habits. "Shoulder peaks before hip" is something you see once the data shows you — then you can't unsee it.

05

Proof, not promises, for parents

Progression charts and KPI comparisons give parents something concrete to look at. Fees get renewed when the chart goes up and the coach is the one who drew the line.

06

Trimmed match footage, ready to review

Watch the point they lost the break. Scrub the five biggest winners. Full trimmed video is linked to the data so you can go from stat to clip in one click, then share it on the group chat.

The workflow

Five minutes to set up.
Five years of value.

1

Player creates their account

Your player (or their parent, for juniors) signs up at ten-fifty5.com. They pick a subscription or start free. Takes 90 seconds.

2

They invite you from the Locker Room

In their dashboard, they add your email under "Invite Coach". You get an email with a secure accept link.

3

You accept. Your coach account is created.

One click on the email link, and you're in. No credit card, no password gymnastics — the token does the auth.

4

You open the portal and see their whole history

Every match, every dashboard, every technique analysis, every AI Coach card. Read-only, obviously — you don't upload, they do.

5

They keep uploading. You keep coaching.

Every new match, new technique clip, new insight card shows up in your portal automatically. You're in the loop without having to chase.
