Match Summary

Serve Placement

Where your serves land,
and where they win.

Every serve plotted on a court heatmap with win-rate breakdowns by zone. Wide, body, T — see what's working and where the free points are hiding.

  • Heatmap dots sized by frequency
  • Win rate by placement zone
  • Deuce / ad court split with 1st vs 2nd filter
Rally Analysis

How long your points last.
And where you drop off.

Rally length distribution, aggression profile (Attack / Neutral / Defence split), depth map and win-rate by rally length. Find the point in your rally where the wheels come off.

  • Length bucketed histogram (1-3, 4-6, 7-9, 10-12, 13+)
  • Aggression pie — are you the aggressor?
  • Win rate by rally length — find your drop-off
Technique Analysis

  • Benchmark vs beginner / intermediate / advanced / pro
